# Demo Documents — Real Public Financial PDFs

Use these freely available, publicly published documents for demonstrations
and management presentations. All are official filings or reports from
publicly listed companies — legal to use for internal demos and testing.

---

## Recommended Documents to Download

### Option 1 — Infosys (Simple, Clean, Well-Structured)

**Annual Report (Balance Sheet + P&L + Cash Flow):**
https://www.infosys.com/investors/reports-filings/annual-report/annual/documents/infosys-ar-23.pdf

Best for: Demonstrating Balance Sheet analysis, P&L summarisation

---

### Option 2 — HDFC Bank Quarterly Results

**Q4 FY2024 Results PDF:**
https://www.hdfcbank.com/content/dam/hdfcbank/investor/hdfc-bank-q4fy24-results.pdf

Best for: Demonstrating Bank Statement analysis, risk scoring

---

### Option 3 — TCS (Tata Consultancy Services)

**Annual Report:**
https://www.tcs.com/content/dam/tcs/investor-relations/financial-statements/2023-24/ar/tcs-annual-report-2023-2024.pdf

Best for: Profit & Loss analysis, financial trend demonstration

---

### Option 4 — NSE India (National Stock Exchange)

**Download any listed company quarterly results:**
https://www.nseindia.com/companies-listing/corporate-filings-financial-results

Steps:
1. Go to the URL above
2. Search for any company (e.g. "Wipro", "Bajaj Finance")
3. Download the PDF under "Financial Results"

---

### Option 5 — BSE India (Bombay Stock Exchange)

**Direct filing search:**
https://www.bseindia.com/corporates/Resultsfinancials.aspx

Steps:
1. Select company name
2. Select "Quarterly Results"
3. Download the PDF

## Recommended Demo Script

For a management demonstration, upload these 3 documents in this order:

1. **Bank Statement equivalent** — use HDFC Q4 results PDF
2. **Balance Sheet** — use Infosys Annual Report
3. **P&L Statement** — use TCS Annual Report

Then demonstrate in this sequence:
1. Upload Documents page — show processing with progress
2. Document Summary — show AI-generated executive summaries
3. Risk Analysis — run and show risk score + radar chart
4. AI Recommendations — generate credit improvement checklist
5. AI Chat — ask: "What is the debt position of this company?"

- All contain only publicly disclosed financial information
- No privacy concerns, no confidential data
